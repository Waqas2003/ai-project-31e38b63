For Every Child

UNICEF works in over 190 countries to save children's lives, to defend their rights, and to help them fulfill their potential.

Our Impact

  • Vaccines

    UNICEF has helped to vaccinate over 3 billion children in the last 20 years.

  • Education

    UNICEF has helped to increase access to education for over 10 million children in the last 5 years.

  • Emergency Response

    UNICEF has responded to over 300 emergencies in the last 10 years, saving countless lives.
